Venue

The workshop will take place at UCLouvain (Université catholique de Louvain), whose main campus is located in Louvain-la-Neuve (BE), a small city 30km to the south-east of Brussels. All events will take place in the mathematical engineering department of UCLouvain (INMA), located in the Euler building (avenue Georges Lemaître, 4, see picture on the right).

The main room for talks will be Euler a002 (on the left after main entrance of the building). It is equipped with a beamer and a large whiteboard.

Louvain-la-Neuve is a very walkable city. You reach the Euler building from the city center by foot (in less than 15 minutes).
