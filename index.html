Information

Welcome to PEP-talks website! In November 16th-17th 2022, we are pleased to hold the first edition of PEP-talks, first seminar exclusively focused on Performance Estimation Problems. It will consist in series of talks and informal discussions around the most recent progresses in the field. It will take place in UC Louvain.

Important Dates

Seminar sessions: Wednesday November 16th and Thursday November 17th.
